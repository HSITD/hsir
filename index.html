About us

HSITD (High-School Student IT Department) is a Voluntary business for high-school students in the uk
that want to learn how to code with various languages! Its completely free and easy to sign up for aswell!

The goals of HSITD is to give students a easy and free way to learn code such as:
Python, Javacript, C++, CSS, HTML and more!
